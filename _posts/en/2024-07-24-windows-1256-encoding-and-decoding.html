Windows-1256, also known as CP-1256, is a character encoding designed to support Arabic script. Primarily used for the Arabic language and other languages that use the Arabic script, such as Arabic, Persian (Farsi), Urdu, Pashto.

Windows-1256 consists of 256 characters, where the first 128 characters are the same as ASCII. The remaining 128 characters (0x80 to 0xFF) include Arabic letters, numerals, and some special symbols.

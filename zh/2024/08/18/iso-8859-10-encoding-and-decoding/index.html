ISO-8859-10 Encoding and Decoding

Published August 18, 2024
By Samuel Yang

ISO-8859-10, also known as Latin-6 or ISO Latin-6, is a character encoding standard from the ISO/IEC 8859 series.

ISO-8859-1 consists of 256 characters, where the first 128 characters are the same as ASCII. The remaining 128 characters cover additional letters, diacritics, and symbols necessary for writing Nordic languages (such as Danish, Norwegian, Swedish, and Finnish).

EUC-CN (GB2312) Encoding and Decoding

Published August 14, 2024
By Samuel Yang

EUC-CN (Extended Unix Code for Simplified Chinese) is a character encoding system designed to represent Simplified Chinese characters in computing environments, especially on Unix-like operating systems. EUC-CN is primarily based on the GB 2312 character set, which is the standard for Simplified Chinese.

EUC-CN (GB2312) uses different byte sequences to represent different types of characters:

  • Single-Byte Characters
    The valid lead byte ranges for single-byte sequences are as follows:
    • 0x00-0x7F
  • Two-Byte Characters
    The valid lead byte ranges for double-byte sequences are as follows:
    • 0xA1-0xFE

    The valid trail byte ranges for double-byte sequences are as follows:
    • 0xA1-0xFE

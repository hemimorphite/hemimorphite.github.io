Windows-1251 (CP-1251) Encoding and Decoding

Published July 24, 2024
By Samuel Yang

Windows-1251 (CP-1251) is a single-byte character encoding designed for Cyrillic script, which is used in various languages of Eastern Europe and Russia. Windows-1251 (CP-1251) is specifically designed to handle Cyrillic alphabets used in languages like Russian, Bulgarian, Serbian, and others.

Windows-1251 consists of 256 characters, where the first 128 characters are the same as ASCII. The remaining 128 characters (0x80 to 0xFF) include Cyrillic letters, punctuation, and other symbols necessary for Russian, Bulgarian, Serbian, Ukrainian, Belarusian, Moldovan.

Windows-1251 also known as CP-1251.
